# Frontend Portfolio Submission

**Role**: Frontend Engineer  
**Purpose**: Showcase of prior work demonstrating frontend engineering and design abilities.

---

## Overview

As part of your application, we’d love to see a few examples of your past frontend projects. These can be professional, open-source, or personal projects — anything that highlights your strengths as an engineer and designer.

We’re especially interested in projects that show **interaction design**, **data visualization**, **API integration**, or **complex UI engineering**.

---

## What to Submit

Please provide the following:

1. **Links to Repositories or Live Projects**
   - GitHub / GitLab repos
   - Live demos or deployed URLs (e.g. Vercel, Netlify, etc.)
   - Screenshots or short videos (if hosted privately)

2. **Short Write-Up for Each Project**
   For each example include a short summary of:
   - **Project Name & Link**
   - **Tech Stack** (frameworks, libraries, build tools, etc.)
   - **Description:** What the project does, who it’s for, what problem it solves
   - **Your Role:** What parts you built or led
   - **Challenges / Highlights:** What makes it technically or creatively interesting
   - **Outcome:** Results, metrics, or what you learned

Example format:

```markdown
### Project: Rate Explorer Dashboard
**Repo:** [github.com/yourname/rate-explorer](https://github.com/yourname/rate-explorer)  
**Stack:** React, TypeScript, D3.js, TailwindCSS  
**Description:** A dashboard for exploring healthcare price transparency data.  
**My Role:** Built the interactive charting components and data-fetching layer.  
**Challenges:** Handling large JSON responses efficiently and building accessible visualizations at million record scale.  
**Outcome:** Improved data rendering speed by 3x; used by a team of 12 internal analysts daily for payer rate review.
```

---

## Evaluation Criteria

When reviewing portfolio projects, we’ll consider:

| Area | What We’re Looking For |
|---|---|
| **UI/UX Craft** | Layout, responsiveness, interactivity |
| **Creativity & Insight** | Thoughtful problem solving |
| **Impact** | Demonstrated usefulness, adoption, or measurable outcome |

---

## Submission

Send your portfolio to [engineering@serifhealth.com](mailto:engineering@serifhealth.com) for review. 

---

## Tips

- Focus on *quality over quantity* — 1–3 well-documented projects are best.  
- Context matters: tell us why you built it, what constraints existed, and how you approached them.  
- Include visuals where possible — screenshots or GIFs help communicate your work instantly.
